About Us

Our History

Pathan Ka Hotel was founded in 1995 with a vision to bring the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of the Pathan culture to the heart of the city. Our journey began in a humble setting, with a few traditional dishes passed down through generations.

Over the years, we have grown, but our commitment to quality, tradition, and providing a memorable dining experience has remained unwavering. We source the finest ingredients and prepare each dish with care and passion, staying true to the rich culinary heritage of the Pathan region.

From our signature kebabs to our aromatic biryanis, every dish tells a story of tradition and flavor. We are proud of our history and look forward to continuing to serve our community with the same dedication and warmth that has defined us for decades.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an authentic and unforgettable culinary experience that celebrates the rich flavors and traditions of Pathan cuisine. We are dedicated to using fresh, high-quality ingredients and preparing each dish with passion and expertise.

We strive to create a welcoming and warm atmosphere where every guest feels like a part of our family. Our goal is to share the taste of our heritage and create lasting memories for our customers through exceptional food and service.
